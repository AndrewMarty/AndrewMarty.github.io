    F.A.Q

    1. What documents are needed for car rental?

      Passport or driver licence

    2. What to do in case of an accident?

      In the event of an accident, call the number specified in the contract

    3. Is it possible to deliver the car to another city?

      Delivery of the car is possible within a radius of 20 km from the city of Nuremberg

    4. Is it possible to transfer the vehicle to third parties?

      Allowed if they are written in the contract

    5. What is not included in the rental price?

      The price does not include fuel

    6. Are there mileage restrictions?

      250 km per day, anything above costs 0.20 cents per kilometer
